PSYCHOLOGICAL MEDICINE

Department of Psychological medicine combines compassionate care and medical expertise to provide comprehensive in-patient and out-patient treatment for psychiatric disorders. Treatment is voluntary and individually tailored ,provided by a multi-disciplinary team consisting of a Psychiatrists, Psychologists, Occupational Therapists and Trained Nurses.
